# Razoreel

**Role:** Fast / High Damage
**Class:** Assassin

## Lore

Once graceful hunters of the mid-waters, Razoreels have been twisted by The Murk into living lightning bolts. Their bodies crackle with corrupted bio-electricity. They don't attack en masse — they slip through gaps in your defenses, targeting weak points with surgical precision. One Razoreel in the wrong place can end a tide.

> "You won't see it coming. You'll only see what it left behind."

|----------|------------------------------------------------------|
| Idle     | Slight wobble, electric sparks flicker along body    |
| Walk     | Fast slithering motion, trail of electric particles  |

| Damaged  | Body flickers, sparks scatter                        |

## Behavior

- Moves quickly along coral paths
- Targets gaps and weak points in defense lines
- Arrives in small packs, not swarms
- Vulnerable to single-target precision weapons (Tideheart Laser)
- Current Cannon can knock them back effectively

## Counters

- **Strong against:** Vibration Zone (moves through too fast for full effect)
- **Weak against:** Tideheart Laser, Current Cannon
