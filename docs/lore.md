# The Abyssal Compact — Lore

## The World

Far beneath the surface where sunlight dies, the **Abyssal Compact** has stood for ten thousand tides. A civilization of mer-people, sea-wraiths, and ancient constructs built their empire around the **Poseidon Citadel** — a fortress of living coral and enchanted stone, powered by the **Tideheart Crystal**, a relic that controls the ocean's currents, temperature, and life itself.

The Citadel is not merely a castle. It is the heartbeat of the deep.

## The Threat — The Feral Surge

For centuries, the deep-sea creatures coexisted with the Compact in uneasy peace. But something stirred in the **Null Trench** — a lightless chasm at the ocean's deepest point. A corrupting force called **The Murk** seeped upward, a dark biological intelligence that rewires the minds of sea creatures, stripping them of instinct and replacing it with a single drive: **destroy the Tideheart Crystal**.

The Feral Surge began without warning. Entire schools turned hostile overnight.

## The Stakes

If the Tideheart Crystal falls, the ocean dies. Currents stop. Cold replaces warmth. Every living thing in the sea — corrupted or not — goes dark.

The Compact's defenders are the last line. The Citadel must hold.

> **The deep remembers. The deep endures.**

## Key Factions

### The Abyssal Compact (Defenders)
- Mer-people, sea-wraiths, and ancient constructs
- Builders of the Poseidon Citadel
- Guardians of the Tideheart Crystal
- Wield crystal-powered weapons and coral-grown defenses

### The Murk (Antagonist Force)
- A dark biological intelligence rising from the Null Trench
- Not a creature — a corrupting force
- Rewires sea life into hostile drones
- Grows stronger with each tide

### The Corrupted (Enemies)
- Formerly peaceful deep-sea creatures
- Razoreels, Stonecrabs, deep-trench octopuses
- No longer have free will — driven by The Murk
- Their destruction is mercy
