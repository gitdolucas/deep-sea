# Arc Spine

**Type:** Chain / Multi-target
**Best Against:** Stoneclaw

## Description

Chains a bio-electric pulse between nearby enemies. The more enemies clustered together, the more devastating the chain. Stoneclaw waves are its prey.

## Upgrade Tiers

| Level | Visual                           | Chains | Behavior                                 |
|-------|-----------------------------------|--------|------------------------------------------|
| L1    | Small spine antenna               | 2      | Chains to 2 targets                      |
| L2    | Taller spine, crystal nodes       | 4      | Chains to 4 targets                      |
| L3    | Full arc array, crackling         | 6      | Chains to 6 targets, leaves burn DoT     |

## Sprite Specs

### Level 1
- Small jagged spine rising from coral base
- Faint electric spark on fire
- Static idle with occasional spark

### Level 2
- Taller spine with crystal amplifier nodes
- Visible chain lightning between targets, radius ring
- Periodic spark pulse idle

### Level 3
- Multi-spine array crackling with constant energy
- Bright chain lightning, pulsing radius ring
- Continuous crackling animation, arcs between spines

## Build Cost

| Action   | Cost (Shells) |
|----------|---------------|
| Build    | Base cost     |
| L1 -> L2 | 2x base cost |
| L2 -> L3 | 4x base cost |

## Strategic Notes

- Devastating against Stoneclaw swarms — chains amplify with enemy density
- At L3, the burn DoT stacks with Vibration Zone's L3 DoT for massive combined damage
- Less effective against lone targets (Razoreel, Colossus)
- Place where paths converge to maximize chain potential
