# Vibration Zone

**Type:** Slow / Utility (Radius)
**Best Against:** Abyssal Colossus

## Description

Resonance emitters embedded in the coral floor pulse with low-frequency waves, disrupting muscle tissue and slowing anything caught in the radius. The Colossus hates these.

## Upgrade Tiers

| Level | Visual                           | Radius | Behavior                                |
|-------|-----------------------------------|--------|-----------------------------------------|
| L1    | Small embedded emitter            | Small  | Mild slow effect                        |
| L2    | Larger emitter, crystal ring      | Medium | Stronger slow effect                    |
| L3    | Pulsing resonance array           | Large  | Slow + damage over time                 |

## Sprite Specs

### Level 1
- Small circular device flush with the coral floor
- Faint ripple rings emanating outward
- Subtle vibration idle

### Level 2
- Larger device with crystal amplifiers around the edge
- Visible slow radius ring on ground, brighter ripples
- Steady pulse idle

### Level 3
- Full resonance array with multiple emitters
- Intense ripple waves, bright pulsing ground effect
- Continuous breathing glow cycle, ground trembles

## Build Cost

| Action   | Cost (Shells) |
|----------|---------------|
| Build    | Base cost     |
| L1 -> L2 | 2x base cost |
| L2 -> L3 | 4x base cost |

## Strategic Notes

- Essential for slowing the Abyssal Colossus, giving DPS towers more time
- At L3, adds damage over time on top of slow — becomes a real threat to Stonecrabs too
- Razoreels move through too fast for full effect at L1 — needs L2+ to matter
- Place at chokepoints for maximum coverage
