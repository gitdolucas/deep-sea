# Ink Veil

**Type:** Debuff / Defensive
**Best Against:** Mixed waves

## Description

A defensive tower that releases a cloud of dark bio-ink, blinding enemies and reducing their accuracy against your Citadel walls.

## Upgrade Tiers

| Level | Visual                             | Coverage | Behavior                                 |
|-------|-------------------------------------|----------|------------------------------------------|
| L1    | Small ink sac on a coral stalk      | Small    | Short-duration blind cloud               |
| L2    | Larger sac, darker ink              | Medium   | Wider cloud, longer duration             |
| L3    | Ink storm generator                 | Large    | Persistent ink storm, also reduces armor |

## Sprite Specs

### Level 1
- Small dark sac mounted on coral stalk
- Releases small puff of dark particles on fire
- Gentle sway idle

### Level 2
- Larger sac with visible ink veins, crystal base
- Darker, wider cloud effect, visible radius ring
- Pulsing sac idle

### Level 3
- Imposing ink storm generator with swirling dark cloud above
- Persistent dark particle field, pulsing radius ring
- Continuous swirl animation, ink dripping

## Build Cost

| Action   | Cost (Shells) |
|----------|---------------|
| Build    | Base cost     |
| L1 -> L2 | 2x base cost |
| L2 -> L3 | 4x base cost |

## Strategic Notes

- Reduces enemy effectiveness — blinded enemies deal less damage to the Citadel
- At L3, armor reduction makes enemies more vulnerable to all other towers
- Works well in combination — place near DPS towers to amplify their damage
- Utility tower, not a direct killer
