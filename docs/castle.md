# Poseidon Citadel

**Role:** Base to defend / Core structure

## Lore

Far beneath the surface where sunlight dies, the Abyssal Compact has stood for ten thousand tides. A civilization of mer-people, sea-wraiths, and ancient constructs built their empire around the Poseidon Citadel — a fortress of living coral and enchanted stone, powered by the Tideheart Crystal, a relic that controls the ocean's currents, temperature, and life itself.

The Citadel is not merely a castle. It is the heartbeat of the deep.

If the Tideheart Crystal falls, the ocean dies. Currents stop. Cold replaces warmth. Every living thing in the sea — corrupted or not — goes dark.

> "The deep remembers. The deep endures."

## Visual Design

The Citadel dominates the back of the map — the focal point of the entire battlefield.

### Structure
- Tall coral and enchanted stone spires rising from the ocean floor
- Arched gates at the entrance where the coral path ends
- Tideheart Crystal embedded at the top — the glowing heart of the fortress
- Living coral growing along walls, slowly pulsing with light

### Color Palette
| Element            | Color                          |
|--------------------|--------------------------------|
| Stone walls        | `#112f55` (deep blue-gray)     |
| Coral accents      | `#1a3a2a` teal + `#ff6b6b` red |
| Tideheart Crystal  | `#00d4ff` (bright cyan)        |
| Window glow        | `#7b2fff` (violet)             |

### Lighting
- The Citadel emits a soft cyan glow from the Tideheart Crystal
- Windows glow violet from inner chambers
- Coral on the walls faintly pulses
- When damaged, the Crystal flickers and dims

## Gameplay

### Health
The Citadel has a health pool. When enemies reach the end of the coral path, they deal damage to the Citadel.

### Damage States
| State      | HP Range  | Visual                                                |
|------------|-----------|-------------------------------------------------------|
| Pristine   | 100-75%   | Full glow, crystal bright, coral vibrant              |
| Damaged    | 74-50%    | Crystal flickers occasionally, cracks in stone        |
| Critical   | 49-25%    | Crystal dims, coral grays out, rubble visible         |
| Near Death | 24-1%     | Crystal barely pulses, structure crumbling, dark      |
| Destroyed  | 0%        | Crystal shatters — game over                          |

### Game Over
When the Citadel falls, the Tideheart Crystal shatters in a dramatic animation — a burst of cyan light followed by all bioluminescence in the scene fading to black. The deep goes dark.

## Position
- Placed at the far end of the map
- All coral paths converge toward it
- Camera angle shows it prominently behind the battlefield
- Its glow serves as a constant visual anchor for the player
